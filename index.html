September, 2024

Solar Energy Fed BLDC motor for Electric Vehicle Applications

Incoporating 3-level diode-lamped Multilevel inverter for the conversion of DC power from solar panel and battery system to a BLDC motor. Incoporating a bidirectional DC-DC converter to charge battery system and increase Electric Vehicle driving range and increase efficiency. Utilizing spartan-6 FPGA board to introduce a closed loop system to reduce torque ripple in BLDC motor via voltage source inverter controller. Simulations performed on MATLAB/SIMULINK. Ongoing work: Implementing V2G and G2V for a microgrid system to improve grid stability.
